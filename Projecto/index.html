About

Welcome to the hauntingly captivating world of Rafael Matias, a 24-year-old artist and musician hailing from the beautiful landscapes of Portugal. With an uncanny ability to blend the realms of visual art and music, Rafael's creations invite you to delve into a realm where darkness meets elegance, and torment intertwines with refinement. At the heart of Rafael's portfolio lies a unique fusion of musical pen and marker drawings and intricately crafted musical compositions, predominantly within the realm of metal. Stepping into his virtual gallery is akin to embarking on a journey through the depths of his creative psyche, where each stroke of the pen and every note played speaks volumes about his artistic vision. Rafael's drawing style is an embodiment of darkness and torment, yet it exudes an undeniable elegance and refinement. With each piece, he invites you to peer into the shadows, where intricate details weave tales of anguish and beauty in equal measure. His art is a symphony of emotions, captivating the observer with its eerie allure and intricate details. In tandem with his visual art, Rafael's musical compositions take you on a sonic voyage unlike any other. Fueled by a desire for heaviness and a penchant for rhythmic and structural complexity, his music transcends boundaries, transporting listeners to realms both familiar and unexplored. Within each composition, echoes of Rafael's artistic sensibilities resound, weaving a tapestry of sound that is as immersive as it is profound. As you explore Rafael Matias's creations, prepare to be mesmerized by the interplay of light and shadow, sound and silence. Whether through his chilling drawings or his thunderous compositions, Rafael invites you to embrace the darkness, to find solace in its depths, and to discover the beauty that lies within.
